About Me


I am a third-year BS-MS student at the UM-DAE Centre for Excellence in Basic Sciences (CEBS), Mumbai. I am interested in cosmology and astrophysics, particularly the study of cosmological probes such as the cosmic microwave background and Type Ia supernovae. I am also interested in gravitational lensing and spacetime-related phenomena. Additionally, I enjoy exploring planetary and stellar atmospheres and their role in determining habitability.
